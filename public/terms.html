DataRelief is a data aggregation platform that surfaces humanitarian crisis information to help donors make informed decisions. We do not process donations directly and are not responsible for third-party donation platforms.

2. Use of the Platform

DataRelief is provided for informational purposes. Crisis data is curated from publicly available sources and verified submissions from organizations. We make no guarantees about the real-time accuracy of any figures.

3. Data Accuracy

All crisis figures include a last-updated date. DataRelief presents data as-of that date. Figures may have changed since collection. We encourage users to verify current conditions through the linked organizations.

4. Third-Party Links

DataRelief links to third-party donation and organizational websites. We are not responsible for the content, practices, or policies of those sites. Attribution tracking is used to measure impact — no personal data is sold.
