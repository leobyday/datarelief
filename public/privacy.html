Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. What we collect

When you click a link from DataRelief to a crisis organization, we log: the crisis ID, organization slug, timestamp, and your browser's user-agent string. This is used solely to measure how many people we connect to humanitarian causes.

2. What we do not collect

We do not collect your name, email, IP address, or any personally identifiable information through normal platform use. We do not use cookies for tracking.

3. Organization submissions

If you submit crisis data on behalf of an organization, we collect the information you provide (organization name, contact email, items needed). This is stored securely and used only to review and publish crisis data.

4. Third parties

DataRelief does not sell, rent, or share any collected data with third parties for advertising purposes. Attribution data (click counts by crisis) may be shared publicly in aggregate form.

5. Contact

Questions? Email contact@datarelief.org.
